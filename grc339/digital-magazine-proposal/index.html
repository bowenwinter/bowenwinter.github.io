Introduction

Pitch & Point

Pitch and Point is a digital magazine for all things Seattle Mariners. “Pitch” comes from pitching in baseball, and “Point” comes from the points of a trident (logo of website). The trident logo is a variation of the old mariners trident logo from the 80s. The website’s target audience is dedicated Mariners fans, especially those with a more technical or in-depth interest in the team. The visual language is largely inspired from Swiss design and Apple—simple and clean.
