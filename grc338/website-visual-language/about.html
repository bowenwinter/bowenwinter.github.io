Welcome to Chesley’s, where great food, refreshing drinks, and rad music come together for a stellar experience. Whether you're here for a casual night out with friends, rattled by the rush, or a special celebration, we offer a welcoming atmosphere for any occasion. Our menu features heavenly appetizers, entrees, drinks and desserts, all crafted with the finest ingredients.

From crispy Chicken Wings tossed in your favorite sauce to our scrumptious Lasagna, hmm. Pair your meal with one of our craft beers or a refreshing cocktail, and you’ve got the perfect recipe for a good time.

With a mix of great tunes and sweet vibes, our sound system fills the air with a carefully curated playlist that makes every moment feel special. It's the perfect place to grab a beer, sing along, and unwind with friends or meet new ones.

In the mouth a dessert! Our delectable Churros, tangy Key Lime Pie, and sweet, sweet, Cheesecake are just a few of the treats that cap off a great meal. Whether you're here for the food, the drinks, or the music, Chesley’s is the place where all your favorites come together!
